---
title: Self-hosting
description: "Host phospho on your own infrastructure"
---

The whole phospho platform can be hosted on your own infrastructure. This is useful if you want to keep your data private or if you have specific data compliance requirements.

The phospho team will handle the deployement, maintenance and continuous updates of the platform on your infrastructure.

# Compatible Cloud Providers

- Google Cloud Platform
- Amazon Web Services
- Microsoft Azure
