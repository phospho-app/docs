## Overview

### How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log">
    **You log** messages (eg: _what the user asked, what the assistant answered_)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze">
    **We run analytics pipelines** on your transcripts. **No OpenAI key needed.** These pipelines can be customized to your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore">
    You explore your LLM app analytics on the **phospho dashboard** and with the integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
