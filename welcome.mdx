## Overview

### How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log">
    **You log the tasks** completed by your LLM app (ex: _what the user asked, what the assistant answered_)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze">
    **We run custom evaluation pipelines** on your transcripts. **No OpenAI key needed.** (ex:
    _detect when the assistant couldn't answer the user's question_)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore">
    You explore your LLM app evaluations on the **phospho dashboard** and with integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Key features

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Logging" href="/getting-started">
    Log every interaction with your LLM app in a non-invasive way. 
    **No monkey patch, no proxy, no framework.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic event detection" href="/guides/events">
    Define events relevant to you, phospho detect them and can warn you
    when they happen. **Take action.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Evaluation" href="/guides/evaluation">
    phospho classify interactions as successes or failures according to
    **your own definition.** 
  </Card>
  <Card title="User Feedback" href="/guides/user-feedback">
    Collect and attach user feedbacks to specific interactions. **All in one place.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Review and Label" href="/guides/evaluation">
    Let your team review and annotate flagged interactions. **Collaborate with non-technical team members.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offline Testing" href="/guides/testing">
    Run tests and get success scores to improve your app before releasing it
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
