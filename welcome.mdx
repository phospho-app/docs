## Overview

### How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log">
    **Log the tasks** completed by your LLM app (ex: _what was the question
    asked by the user, and what answer of the assistant_)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run evals">
    Configure the analytics pipeline to **run evaluations** on transcripts (ex:
    _detect when the assistant couldn't answer the user's question_)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore">
    Explore your LLM app evaluations on the **phospho dashboard**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Key features

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Logging">
    Log every interactions with your LLM app in a non-invasive way
  </Card>
  <Card title="User Feedback">
    Collect and attach user feedbacks to specific interactions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Review and Annotate">
    Let your team review and annotate flagged interactions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Evaluation">
    phospho will classify interactions as a success or a failure according to
    **your** own definition of success
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic event detection">
    Define events relevant to you, phospho will detect them and can warn you
    when they happen
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offline Testing">
    Run tests and get success scores to improve your app before releasing it
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
